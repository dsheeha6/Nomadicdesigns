What is Callback Automation?

Callback automation is a system that instantly calls or texts a lead as soon as they fill out a form or request contact. These tools integrate with your CRM or website and automatically route leads to sales reps based on availability, location, or product.

Imagine this: someone submits a "Get a Quote" form, and your phone rings with the lead already on the line. That's callback automation at work.
